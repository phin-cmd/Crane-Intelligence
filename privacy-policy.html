CRANE INTELLIGENCE ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Personal Information

We collect the following types of personal information when you use our services:

  • Account Registration: Full name, email address, company name, and password

  • Newsletter Subscriptions: Email address, first name, last name, company name, and subscription preferences
  • Authentication: JWT access tokens and refresh tokens stored in your browser's local storage
  • User Preferences: Subscription tier, account settings, and notification preferences

Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your use of our services:

  • Device information and IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • User agent strings for security and analytics
  • Pages visited and time spent on our platform
  • Crane valuation requests and report generation activity
  • Error logs and performance data
  • Form submission timestamps and source information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and maintain our crane valuation and reporting services
  • Process consultation requests and send confirmation emails
  • Manage user accounts and authenticate access to the platform
  • Send newsletter communications and marketing materials (with your consent)
  • Send technical notices, support messages, and service updates
  • Respond to your inquiries submitted through contact forms
  • Notify admin users of new consultation requests
  • Improve our platform functionality and develop new features
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except in the following circumstances:

Important: We never share your personal crane data, valuation information, or consultation requests with third parties without your explicit consent. Your data is stored securely in our database and is only accessible to authorized admin users for the purpose of responding to your requests.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:
