Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and similar technologies

Last Updated: January 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and enabling certain functionality.

We use both first-party cookies (set by us) and third-party cookies (set by other services we use) to enhance your experience on our platform.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration Essential
Essential Cookies Enable basic website functionality, login, and security Session / 1 year Yes
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how visitors use our website 2 years No
Preference Cookies Remember your settings and preferences 1 year No
Marketing Cookies Used for targeted advertising and marketing 1 year No

3. Specific Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Our platform uses browser local storage (not traditional cookies) for essential functionality:

  • access_token: JWT authentication token stored in localStorage to maintain your login session
  • refresh_token: Token used to refresh your authentication session
  • user_data: Stores your user profile information for display purposes
  • Session Management: Browser session data for maintaining authentication state
Note: Our platform primarily uses browser localStorage for authentication tokens rather than traditional HTTP cookies. This provides enhanced security and user control over authentication data.

Analytics Cookies

We may use analytics services that set cookies to help us understand how visitors use our website:

  • _ga: Google Analytics - distinguishes users (if enabled)
  • _gid: Google Analytics - distinguishes users (if enabled)
  • _gat: Google Analytics - throttles request rate (if enabled)

Preference Cookies

We may store user preferences in browser storage:

  • Newsletter Preferences: Your subscription preferences and email communication settings
  • Cookie Preferences: Your cookie consent choices
  • User Settings: Any customizations you make to your account

4. Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that may set cookies or use similar technologies on our website:

  • Email Service Providers: For sending consultation confirmations and newsletter communications (Brevo/Sendinblue)
  • Payment Processors: For processing subscription payments (if applicable)
  • Analytics Services: For understanding website usage and improving our services

These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie practices. We recommend reviewing their policies for more information. We only share data necessary for these services to function.

  • Configure your browser settings

6. Browser Settings

You can also control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Important: Disabling certain cookies may affect your experience on our website. Essential cookies are required for basic functionality and cannot be disabled.

If you disable cookies, you may experience:

  • Inability to log in or maintain your session
  • Loss of personalized settings and preferences
  • Reduced functionality of interactive features
  • Inability to complete transactions
